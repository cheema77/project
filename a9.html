A9 Compact

Inspired by the compact cars popular in Europe and Japan, the A9 may be the smallest car in the line-up, but it is certainly big on style. Featuring smaller 3-spoke wheels, low-profile tires and a generous roofline with plenty of headroom, your child is sure to get great mileage from this little gem. Ages 3+
